Uncle's bike | Digital Narrative

Topic and conflict

Several important themes and topics intersect in the digital narrative. The central topics are the waste of time and energy. The protagonist experience internal crisis when riding on public transport as it takes his time and enthusiasm. Crowd, noise and slow commute create a serious challenge for the main character. The conflict reinforces and forces the main character to surrender due to the situation.

Final thoughts

The creation and the development of the digital narrative was a great challenge as well as experience for me. I was able to demonstrate and apply my creativity, skills to build a powerful movie.
